- Lines: Format as "{SIZE} {SERVICE_NAME} line {NUMBER}-{LOCATION}" (e.g., "8\" Firewater line 0491-018100-X")

ISA 5.1 INSTRUMENT DECODING:
First Letter (Variable): P=Pressure, T=Temperature, F=Flow, L=Level, A=Analysis, H=Hand, etc.
Second Letter (Function): A=Alarm, T=Transmitter, I=Indicator, S=Switch, V=Valve, C=Control
Examples:
- PA = Pressure Alarm
- PT = Pressure Transmitter
- PI = Pressure Indicator
- HS = Hand Switch
- HV = Hand Valve (or Hydraulic Valve)
- PSV = Pressure Safety Valve
- XOV = Solenoid Operated Valve

SERVICE CODES FOR LINES:
FW=Firewater, IA=Instrument Air, PA=Plant Air, SW=Service Water, ST=Steam, FG=Fuel Gas, N2=Nitrogen
